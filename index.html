    Jaguar, Berlin

    In opinion of many techno fans, especially the younger ones, Berlin is the center of techno music. ... Techno originated from Detroit, it was developed there in late 80s. In opinion of many, new machine sound was also inspired by German band Kraftwerk. Techno came to West Germany at the very end of 1980s. And then the Jaguar oppend.

    First oppend on 19.08.1992

    As one of the first techno clubs in Berlin we opened in Summer of 1992. We hosted some of the best world DJs and seen a lot since then. Troughout the years Jaguar became something like a techno Meka.
